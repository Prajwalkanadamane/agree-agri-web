💰 PM-KISAN Samman Nidhi

Under this scheme, an income support of ₹6,000 per year is provided to all farmer families across the country in three equal installments.

Check Your Status:

1. Go to the official website: pmkisan.gov.in

2. Click on "Know Your Status".

⚠️ Beware of Frauds: The government will never call you asking for OTPs to release PM-KISAN funds.

⚠️ The "Equipment Instead of Cash" Scam

DBT Violation

The Lie: Officials tell you that instead of subsidy money, you must accept highly-priced equipment.

Your Right: If a scheme is a Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT), the government mandates the money goes directly into your bank account.

⚠️ The "Missing Parts" Scam

Subsidy Theft

The Lie: You are given an incomplete government kit.

Your Right: Never sign the receiving document until you have the complete kit as listed on the official portal.
